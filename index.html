Description

IntresTools helps you plan smarter money moves—free and offline.

Calculate monthly loan payments and full amortization, compare two loans side-by-side, test refinance savings and break-even, preview home-equity payments within an 80% LTV cap, and model investment growth with or without reinvesting interest. Everything runs on your device—no accounts, no tracking.

What you can do

  • Loan: monthly payment, principal vs interest, full schedule
  • Compare: A/B loans with monthly cost, total interest & balance curves
  • Refinance: break-even rate, fees, and monthly change
  • Home Equity: keep ≥20% equity (80% LTV cap), see combined payments
  • Investment: contributions vs interest, reinvest toggle & charts

Privacy & trust

All calculations run locally on your device. We don’t collect personal data.

Disclaimer

Results are estimates for education and planning and aren’t financial advice. Check terms with your lender or advisor.
